    Historical Note

    The Surgical Research Society was formed in 1915. In its early years, meetings were held biannually, but later a system of two day yearly meetings was adopted. Members hailed from Baltimore, Boston, Philadelphia, and New York City, as well as other cities on a case by case basis. Meetings were held in these cities, with the chosen city's members acting as host.

    Collection Description

    Scope and Content

    This small collection primarily contains minutes, meeting programs, correspondence, membership lists, and other records of the Society. A letter concerning the transfer of these records to the New York Academy of Medicine is also included.

    Arrangement of Collection

    The records are arranged chronologically.

    The original folders in which the records were stored have been discarded. Prior to disposal, copies of the front of the folders were made and placed with the corresponding records.

    Using this Collection
