Biographical Note

Historical Statement

Street and city guides were distributed by doctors as a means to advertise their medical services. Many of the practices specialized in men's health and sexually transmitted diseases.

Collection Description

Scope and Content

Most of the pamphlets are as much about health and medical services as street listings. Some include other local information such as railroad fares, building guides, and calendars or baseball schedules (used to estimate publication dates).
