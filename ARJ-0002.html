Historical Note

The John B. Deaver Surgical Society was a society for students at the University of Pennsylvania's School of Medicine.

Collection Description

Scope and Content

A register for the members of the John B. Deaver Surgical Society. Elements include: name, class of, place of birth, previous education, degrees, date of matriculation, date of entrance into the society, hospital appointments, personal address, and remarks. There are 175 entries, many of them partially filled out.

Additional Material

Additional records from the John B. Deaver Surgical Society can be found in the College of Physicians of Philadelphia's Historical Collections.
